Touch Targets

  • Primary, skip, save, and apply buttons stay at minimum 40px high.
  • Icon buttons stay 36 x 36px only when visual hit area is clear.
  • Inputs remain 40px high with visible focus state.

Collapsing Strategy

  • Top nav collapses to essential actions below 720px.
  • Hero stays a single-column catalog intro.
  • Feature and product grids reduce columns rather than scaling cards down.
  • Job cards are listed vertically so users can scroll through all roles without stacking interactions.
  • Phone-container views hide visible scrollbars but preserve touch scrolling.
